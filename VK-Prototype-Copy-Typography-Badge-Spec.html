VK Prototype Copy, Typography & Badge Spec

What to call it: VK Prototype Copy, Typography & Badge Spec (short: type & badge spec). Browser file: this page · April 2026

This is the browser-readable spec (fonts, bold, caps, colors, badges). Markdown twin: VK-Prototype-Copy-Typography-Badge-Spec.md.

Two badge looks. The 10-page desktop prototype uses filled grid pills (.grid-badge in VintageKing-Redesign-v2_67-Mar2026.html). The mic category lab uses outline tags (.pcard-badge in VK-Microphones-CategoryDemo-v4-refined.html). Both are correct for their file—do not mix them on the same tile without a decision.

When to use bold

Use boldDo not bold
<strong> for rare emphasis in long copy (e.g. All filters).Whole sentences or SEO stuffing.
Checked filter row: weight 600 + red.Unchecked rows: 400.
Current price: 500.“Was” price: 400, strikethrough.

ALL CAPS

Only for labels (eyebrows, drawer <summary>, quick row labels, badge text). Product titles stay Title Case. Badges are always uppercase in the UI.
